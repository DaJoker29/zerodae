Mmm, Yes, Quite

The shop is still open but the newsletter is currently on a hiatus.

SunflowerDM

In 2017, I started playing Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition. Since then, I've run multiple campaigns and countless one-shots for friends and strangers alike.

In 2024, I created SunflowerDM to share the homebrewed campaign setting I've been building over the years, along with some of the tips and tricks I've picked up from several years as a player and Dungeon Master.

I plan on publishing the campaign setting and a compendium of adventures based there. In the meantime, check out the free Heart of Stone adventure for a taste of the what I've been cooking up.
